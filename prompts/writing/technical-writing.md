
# Technical Writing Prompt

## Prompt

"Write a short technical article (300-500 words) explaining the difference between VMs and containers. The article should be targeted at beginners with a basic understanding of IT concepts.

The article should cover the following points:
- What is a virtual machine (VM)?
- What is a container?
- What are the key differences between VMs and containers in terms of architecture, performance, and portability?
- When would you choose to use a VM over a container, and vice versa?

Please use clear and simple language, and include an analogy to help explain the concepts (e.g., the house vs. apartment analogy)."
